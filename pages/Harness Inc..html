Harness Inc. is a software company that provides a modern software delivery platform, primarily focused on continuous integration (CI), continuous delivery (CD), feature flagging, cloud cost management, security testing orchestration, and related DevOps capabilities. Founded in 2016 by Jyoti Bansal (previously founder of AppDynamics), Harness aims to simplify and automate the process of building, testing, deploying, and optimizing applications in the cloud and on-premises environments.

The key differentiator for Harness lies in its strong emphasis on automation and artificial intelligence. The platform claims to eliminate many of the manual and error-prone tasks in CI/CD pipelines, such as automated canary deployments, rollback, verification using machine learning, and continuous security checks. Harness positions itself as a platform that prioritizes developer velocity—letting teams ship software faster, more reliably, and with less need for dedicated DevOps resources.

Compared to traditional CI/CD tools like Jenkins or open-source platforms like Argo CD, Harness offers a more integrated, user-friendly, and visual interface, along with built-in governance, security, and cost management tools. Unlike point solutions that only address parts of the delivery lifecycle, Harness aspires to provide an all-in-one platform with both breadth and depth across the software delivery process.
